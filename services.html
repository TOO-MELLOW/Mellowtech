Our Services

Full-Scope Solutions

Building Every Type of Project

BaobabBuild offers a comprehensive range of construction and engineering services to clients across the Limpopo province. Whether you're a government department, a private developer, or a homeowner — we have the capacity, credentials, and expertise to deliver.

Our CIDB Grade 7 registration allows us to take on contracts up to R40 million across both building and civil engineering categories.

Service 01

Commercial Construction

We deliver premium commercial buildings for the Limpopo business community — from ground-up office parks to retail centres, warehouses, and mixed-use developments. Our commercial division manages everything from initial site preparation to the final handover.

  • Office parks and corporate headquarters
  • Retail centres and shopping complexes
  • Industrial warehouses and factories
  • Hotels and hospitality facilities
  • Mixed-use and multi-storey developments

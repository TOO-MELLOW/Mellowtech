Password Reset Service

Locked out? We'll get you back in – securely and with proof of ownership.

We help you regain access to your devices

Whether you've forgotten your Windows login, your Mac password, or the pattern on your Android – we can help. Our password reset service is secure, fast, and requires proof of ownership to ensure your data stays safe.

  • Windows (all versions)
  • Mac (macOS)
  • Android (pattern, PIN, password)
  • iOS (iPhone/iPad passcode – only with proof of purchase)

How It Works

1

Bring your device

Visit our shop with the locked device and a valid ID.

2

Proof of ownership

We'll ask for proof of purchase or other verification to ensure you're the owner.

3

We reset the password

Using specialised tools, we remove the old password. You set a new one.

Important: Security First

We take your privacy and security seriously. We will never reset a password without verifying that you are the legitimate owner. This helps prevent unauthorised access.

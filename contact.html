Quick Answers

Frequently asked
questions

How long does a typical job take?
Most jobs are completed same-day. Windows installations take 1–2 hours. PC repairs vary depending on the issue, but we always give you a realistic time estimate upfront before starting any work.
Will my data and files be safe?
Absolutely. Data preservation is our first priority on every single job. We back up where possible and take every precaution to ensure nothing is lost — we have a 100% data preservation record across all jobs.
How do I know what it will cost before I commit?
We give you a clear, honest quote before we start any work. No hidden fees, no surprise charges. You agree to the price first — we only begin once you're satisfied with the quote and the plan.
Do you offer remote or on-site services?
We offer both. Many software, Windows, and Office jobs can be handled remotely via secure screen sharing. Hardware-related repairs and diagnostics will typically require the device in person.
What if something isn't working after the job?
We stand fully behind our work. If something we fixed isn't working correctly afterwards, contact us and we'll resolve it — at no additional charge for issues directly related to the work we did.
Do you help businesses, not just individuals?
Yes — we work with businesses of all sizes. Our Business Digital Setup service is specifically designed for companies needing end-to-end digital transformation, complete tool setup, and hands-on staff training.

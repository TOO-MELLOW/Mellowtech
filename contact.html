Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you operate in?

We are based in Polokwane and operate throughout the entire Limpopo province — including Tzaneen, Phalaborwa, Lephalale, Musina, Louis Trichardt, and surrounding areas.

How long does it take to get a quote?

We aim to provide detailed BOQ-based quotes within 5 to 7 business days of receiving all project information and completing a site inspection.

Are you registered on the CSD/Tender Portal?

Yes. BaobabBuild is fully registered on the Central Supplier Database (CSD) and all major government tender portals in Limpopo and nationally.

What is your CIDB grading?

We hold a CIDB Grade 7 in both Building (GB7) and Civil Engineering (CE7), enabling us to tender for contracts up to R40 million.

Do you handle project financing?

We work with a number of accredited financiers and can assist clients in connecting with development finance institutions for larger projects.

Can I visit your offices?

Absolutely. Our offices at 12 Thabo Mbeki Street, Polokwane are open Monday to Friday 07:30–17:00. We welcome clients to visit and discuss projects in person.

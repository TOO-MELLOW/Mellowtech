Who We Are

Built on Trust & Local Knowledge

BaobabBuild has been shaping the skyline of Polokwane and the wider Limpopo province since 2008. We are a proudly South African, black-owned construction company with deep roots in our community.

Our team of qualified engineers, project managers, and skilled tradespeople delivers every project on time, within budget, and to the highest national standards.

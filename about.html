About BaobabBuild

Our History

Rooted in Limpopo

BaobabBuild was founded in 2008 by a group of qualified engineers and construction professionals who saw an opportunity to deliver world-class building services to the people of Limpopo — from the inside out.

We began with a single residential project in Polokwane's Bendor suburb. Today, we manage multi-million rand government and commercial contracts across the entire province, from Polokwane to Musina, from Tzaneen to Lephalale.

As a 100% black-owned company, we are deeply committed to empowerment, skills development, and reinvesting in the communities we serve. The baobab tree — iconic to Limpopo — represents exactly who we are: deep roots, long life, and strength that endures.

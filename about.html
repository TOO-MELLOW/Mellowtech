About MellowTech

We're more than just technicians – we're your neighbours who love tech and people.

How It All Started

Founded in 2015 by two friends who shared a passion for technology and community, MellowTech began as a tiny repair shop in Observatory. We believed that tech support should be friendly, transparent, and stress‑free.

Over the years we've grown, but our core values haven't changed. We still greet every customer with a smile, explain things in plain language, and treat your devices as if they were our own. Whether you're a student with a broken laptop or a business that needs data recovered, we're here for you.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Honest diagnoses, fair pricing. We never recommend repairs you don't need.

Warmth

No intimidating tech speak. We make you feel comfortable and informed.

Expertise

Continuous learning, certified technicians, and years of hands-on experience.

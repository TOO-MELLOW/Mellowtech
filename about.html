Who We Are

We started small.
We stayed honest.

Mellow Tech Services was born out of a simple observation: the people who needed tech help the most — students, small business owners, everyday South Africans — were the ones being left behind. Too expensive. Too confusing. Too impersonal.

We started with a laptop, a promise, and a belief that tech services should be accessible, transparent, and genuinely useful. Every service we offer was added because a real person needed it.

"We don't just fix your tech — we make sure you understand it, own it, and feel confident using it."

Today, with 500+ happy clients and 10+ professional services, we still operate with that same first-day energy. No shortcuts. No inflated quotes. No disappearing after payment is received.
